How to play

Objective:

Clean up the Great Pacific Garbage Patch, rescue marine life, and recycle correctly to restore the ocean ecosystem and earn points!

How to Play:

1. Settings: Access the settings anytime via the top-right corner icon.

2. Start: Click "Start" on the main menu to begin. Each day lasts 5 minutes.

3. Map & Missions: Red exclamation points on the map show areas that need help. Click to begin missions in that area.

4. Game Area:

Collect Items: Green-boxed items go to your inventory. White-boxed items are interactive but not collectible.

Track Progress: Use the mission bar on the right side to monitor progress.

Learn: Clicking on items reveals environmental facts.

5. Minigame: After completing missions or when time runs out, play the recycling minigame by dragging items from your inventory to the correct bin.

Correct sorting earns points; incorrect sorting loses points.

New objects unlock as your points increase.

6. End of Day: View your stats, including progress and unlocked items. Click "Continue" for the next day or "End" to return to the main menu.

Objects:

Garbage: Collect and recycle later.

Coral: Click to plant them; they stay on the screen.

Animals: Click to rescue them—they’ll thank you and leave.

Oil: Click to clean spills—they’ll disappear.
